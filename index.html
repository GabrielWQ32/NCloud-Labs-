// 02 About

The Engineer Behind
NCloud Labs

Gabriel Weinstein Qualls is a cloud infrastructure specialist and the founder of NCloud Labs. With academic grounding from Kennesaw State University in technology — and hands-on exposure to cloud-focused research initiatives in collaboration with engineers from Georgia Tech's technical community — Gabriel brings a rare combination of rigorous theory and real-world implementation.

His work centers on one belief: that small and mid-sized businesses are consistently underserved by the cloud industry — either priced out of quality consulting or handed cookie-cutter solutions by firms that don't understand their actual scale. NCloud Labs was built to close that gap.

Gabriel's security-first mindset — shaped by formal certifications in cybersecurity and cloud architecture — means every architecture he designs is hardened by default, not as an afterthought. Whether it's a first-time cloud migration or a cost optimization overhaul of an existing AWS environment, the approach is always custom, always transparent, and always priced to compete with — and beat — traditional IT teams.
